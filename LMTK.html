LMTK

The hell is this?

LMTK, or LuraMoth Tool Kit is a whole bunch of tools made for just about the only way a Luramoth tool could ever be made for, the niche.

The point of LMTK is to give people niche tools that would otherwise never exist, just because your problem is unusual that doesn't mean you shouldn't have the tools to solve it, that what the point of this is.

now while all this is cool, thing is I can only really make tools that appeal to my own personal niches, thats where I need your help.

Developers everywhere!
